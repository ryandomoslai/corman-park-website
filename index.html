About CPVS:


We are a thriving mixed-animal practice located five miles west of Saskatoon on highway fourteen. The clinic is a multi-veterinarian practice treating all types of animals. We have a full service small and large animal clinic, and mobile veterinary units serving our clients that are unable to bring their animals to us. The clinic has been established since the early 1970’s, and we are fortunate to have gained a large and faithful clientele. We continue to grow and welcome new clients who are drawn to our clinic for our down-home practicality, honest approach to problem solving, and deep rooted compassion for all animals.

We help all kinds of animals!

Small Animal

Our trained staff and veterinarians will, through direct consultation with you, seek to find the best solution for your ailing pets and will be able to guide you through the daily challenges of raising healthy long living companions. We have in house blood analysis, diagnostic and dental x-ray, ultrasound, a complete surgical suite, facilities for isolation of contagious pets, as well as hospitalization of sick pets. We are happy to help with all your small animal vet needs!

Large Animals

CPVS originated and was built on the backs of our loyal large animal clients. We are rooted in the traditional rural Saskatchewan ethos and always seek to provide veterinary care reflective of the values that we see in our clients. We provide the many services required by a wide range of farm species. We attend to a diverse clientele, from small family farms to large production type operations. Our veterinarian team is able to do many on farm surgical procedures and diagnostic examinations, however a full service large animal clinic exists to accommodate those who wish to bring their animals to the clinic.
